How To Protect Yourself

You should do

  • Stay at home
  • Wear mask properly
  • Use alcohol based sanitizer
  • Maintain proper hygiene
  • Wash your hands frequently
  • Frequent self-isolation

You should avoid

  • Contact with sick people
  • Public gathering
  • Handshaking
  • Touching surfaces
  • Touching your face
  • Stepping out unless necessary
